Precision & Quality

A Decade of Precision Engineering & Manufacturing Mastery

Welcome to BestChem Technology, backed by the robust heritage of Siddheshwar Tools & Components. Based in the industrial hub of Bhosari, Pune, we have established ourselves as the premier destination for advanced injection mould manufacturing and precision VMC machining.

Over the course of 10+ years, our operations have expanded from a modest tooling setup to a comprehensive engineering firm capable of taking complex moulding challenges from concept to realization. Our multi-disciplinary team is highly skilled in crafting precision components that form the backbone of modern industrial machinery across multiple sectors.

  • Quality First Approach

    Strict adherence to tolerances and high-grade material selection ensuring flawless output every time.

  • Advanced Technologies

    Leveraging state-of-the-art VMC machines and modern 3D tooling techniques to eliminate bottlenecks.

Our Mission

To provide robust, highly accurate injection moulding and engineering solutions that exceed industry expectations, enabling our clients to build superior products while optimizing manufacturing speed and cost.

Our Vision

To consistently be recognized as the leading and most trusted partner in precision tooling and machining for top-tier industrial projects across the country.
